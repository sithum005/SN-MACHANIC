Our Specialized Services

Expert repair and maintenance for a wide range of industrial and domestic sewing machines.

Standard

Singer Single Needle

The classic workhorse for standard sewing. We provide complete diagnostics, timing adjustments, tension calibration, and motor servicing for all Singer single needle models.

  • Needle bar alignment
  • Feed dog height adjustment
  • Belt tensioning & replacement
Heavy Duty

Zoje Industrial Machine

Built for speed and durability, Zoje industrial machines require precise tuning. Our technicians are factory-trained to handle the complex mechanics of these high-performance units.

  • Oil pump system flushing
  • High-speed timing balance
  • Electronic control box repairs
Compact

Baby Overlock

Delicate yet essential for finishes. We specialize in loop timing and blade sharpening for baby overlock machines to ensure clean, professional edges.

  • Upper & lower looper timing
  • Blade sharpening & replacement
  • Thread tension optimization
Industrial

Zoje 5 Overlock

The 5-thread safety stitch machine is critical for garment construction. We ensure all five threads interact perfectly for a strong, beautiful seam.

  • Complex 5-thread synchronization
  • Differential feed adjustment
  • Chain stitch mechanism repair
